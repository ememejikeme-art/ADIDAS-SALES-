# ADIDAS-SALES
## RECOMMENDATIONS FOR ADIDAS
### PRODUCT STRATEGY
- Adidas should focus and invest more in men’s foot wear and women’s Apparel since they are the most revenue generating product categories and produce little accessories that are low performing like women athlete footwear.
### CHANNEL OPTIMIZATION
- Adidas should increase online sales through targeted digital marketing initiatives, enhance online shopping experience and also enhance in-store shopping experience in high-traffic retail locations.
### REGIONAL FOCUS
- Adidas should increase investment in West and Northeast regions since they are the high performing regions, this can be done by opening more branches in these regions.
### DEMAND FORECASTING
- Supply should be focused strictly on product demands and inventory should be properly managed to avoid out-of-stock situations.
### RETAILER AND PARTNER MANAGEMENT
- Adidas should build stronger relationships with top performing retailers, optimize relationships with key distributors like foot Locker and also renegotiate relationships and contracts with underperforming partners.
